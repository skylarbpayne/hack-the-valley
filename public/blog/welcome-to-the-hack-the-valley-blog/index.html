Welcome to the Hack the Valley blog

The first Hack the Valley proved something simple: there are people in Bakersfield and Kern County who want to build, and who show up when you give them a reason to. Students, mentors, organizers, sponsors — a whole room of them.

This blog is where we keep that momentum going between events. Expect event recaps, student project highlights, behind-the-scenes notes from organizing, and honest updates on what we are trying next.

What you will find here

  • Recaps — what happened, what worked, and what we learned.
  • Project spotlights — the things people actually built.
  • Community updates — workshops, meetups, and ways to get involved.

That is the whole idea. No fluff — just the useful stuff when there is something real to share.
