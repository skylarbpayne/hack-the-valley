Project submissions

Submit your Hack the Valley project.

One form. Upload screenshots and a demo video, add your links, and save the confirmation ID after submit.

Before you submit

  • Have your project title, team members, and track ready.
  • Add a demo/deployed URL if you have one.
  • Add GitHub/repo and slide links if available.
  • Upload up to 5 images and one demo video.
Upload limits
Images: 15 MB each. Video: 500 MB. If campus Wi‑Fi gets cursed, paste a YouTube/Loom/Drive link too.
